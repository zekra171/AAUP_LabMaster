Frequently asked questions (FAQ) :

  • How do I book a lab?
    Select the lab you want to book, choose the equipment (if needed), pick a date and time, and submit your request, “Book now”
  • Can I cancel or modify my booking?
    Yes, you can cancel or edit your booking you can contact the lab administrator through the "Contact Us" page for assistance.
  • What should I do if I don't find an available time?
    If no times are available, you can contact the lab administrator through the "Contact Us" page for assistance.
  • What kind of equipment can I book?
    Available equipment at our labs.
  • How will I know if my booking is confirmed?
    You will receive a confirmation email once your booking is reviewed and approved by the lab administrator.
  • Are there any rules for using the labs?
    Yes, you must follow the lab's safety and usage guidelines, which are provided in the "Lab Rules" section.
  • What happens if I miss my booked session?
    If you miss your session without prior notice, it may affect your ability to make future bookings.
  • Can I book the lab for group work?
    Yes, but you must specify the number of group members and the purpose of the booking in the "Purpose" field.
  • Who should I contact if I encounter technical issues?
    For technical support, contact us via email at labsupport@aaup.edu or through the "Contact Us" page.
